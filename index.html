Who is Nicolas Cage Really?

Who really is Nicolas Cage? Some know him from his dashing role as the Beast, in the movie Beauty and the Beast. Others from his more delicate roles in movies such as The Sound of Music, and his voice acting as Bambi. However, this year Mr. Cage has really outdone himself with five blockbuster films. Starting with Interstellar where Mr. Cage leads a team of explorers who travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanitys survial.

Shortly after Mr. Cage showed his lightning speed in the Maze Runner, which led many to reflect on his groundbreaking film The Running Man. Only a month after filming the Maze Runner, Mr. Cage played the role of Michelangelo, a giant mutant turtle in a fan favorite The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

Final with a month left in the year Mr Cage pulled together another fantastic film staring as Root the giant tree in the film Guardians of the Galaxy.
